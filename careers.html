Careers

Casino Game Maker Inc. is a global leader in the design, development and manufacturing of gaming machines. We are always looking for the best and the brightest to enhance our team. Our employees drive our success, and we are committed to recruiting and retaining the most talented and inventive team members dedicated to our core values.

Software

Our software team brings our games to life. We are looking for developers who have experience with object-oriented languages in general or are familiar with any of the following languages: C++, C#, Java, Haxe, or AS3. We are also looking for programmers who have experience creating and maintaining servers and databases.
